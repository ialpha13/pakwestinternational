Pakwest favicon package

Included:
- favicon.ico with 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 128, and 256 px sizes
- Browser PNG favicons from 16x16 to 512x512
- Apple touch icons, including apple-touch-icon.png
- Android Chrome icons and maskable icons
- Windows/Microsoft tile icons
- site.webmanifest
- browserconfig.xml
- favicon-html-snippet.txt

How to use:
1. Upload the files to your website root/public folder.
2. Copy the tags from favicon-html-snippet.txt into the <head> section of your website.
3. Update site.webmanifest name and short_name from “Your Website” to your real website name.
