## Page targeting
- `/` (Home): manufacturer + exporter + wholesale intent
- `/products`: product catalog + sizes/variations + wholesale intent
- `/products/{category}`: category-level wholesale intent
- `/products/{category}/{product}`: product-level bulk buying intent
- `/categories`: discovery keywords for lamps, tiles, edible, lick blocks
- `/quality`: trust keywords (99.9% NaCl, ISO 9001, FDA approved)
- `/about`: brand trust + exporter/company profile intent
- `/contact`: quote/sample/private label conversion intent
